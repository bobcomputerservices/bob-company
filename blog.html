AutoCount Loading List Plugin

Nov 12, 2022


AutoCount Loading List Plugin, is specially developed based on our customers’ business needs. With customizable filters such as delivery area, delivery date, and more, it generates a clear loading list — making it easier to load goods and plan deliveries accurately.

AutoCount 装货清单插件 (Loading List Plugin)，是我们根据客户的实际业务需求特别开发的。用户可通过自定义条件（如:送货地区、送货日期）快速生成装货清单，方便安排货物装载与配送，更加省时省力。
