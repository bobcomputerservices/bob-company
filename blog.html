AutoCount Weighing System Plugin

Introducing the AutoCount Weighing System Plugin, designed to simplify and automate your weighing operations. It seamlessly connects to your weighing system while a loaded lorry is being unloaded. The plugin automatically calculates the net weight and generates a Cash Purchase or Purchase Order based on the value of the unloaded goods. Boost accuracy, save time, and streamline your entire weighing-to-billing process.

全新推出 AutoCount 过磅系统插件，专为简化和自动化您的过磅操作而设计。它可以无缝连接到您的过磅系统，在罗里卸货过程中自动工作。插件会自动计算净重量，并根据卸货货物的价值生成现金采购单或采购订单。提升精准度、节省时间，让您的过磅到结算流程更加高效顺畅。
