"你的电脑，他的希望”二手电脑捐赠计划

Jan 28, 2021

As the pandemic continues, attending school in person has become a major challenge, while online learning has emerged as an essential mode of education. However, many underprivileged students from low-income families lack access to computers and stable internet connections, causing them to miss out on online classes and gradually drift away from the formal education system. In light of this, and out of concern for children’s education and their basic rights, the Panchor State Assemblyman’s Office, the Batu Pahat Chinese Media Association, the Lions Club of Batu Pahat, and the Computer Traders Association Batu Pahat have jointly launched the “Your Computer, Their Hope” Second-hand Computer Donation Program. This initiative aims to help underprivileged students obtain second-hand computers and tablets, enabling them to participate in online learning from home and enjoy equal educational opportunities.

疫情下，不要让任何一个孩子在教育路上掉队! 疫情当前，去学校上课成了一件难以完成的任务，而网课在目前趋势下成了一种重要教育渠道。然而，不少贫穷线下的基层学童因未有足够电脑设备及网路，导致孩子失去上网课及线上学习的机会，从正规教育体系中慢慢脱轨。关注学童教育及基本权益，帆加兰州议员办公室、峇株巴辖华文媒体协会、峇株巴辖狮子会、峇株巴辖电脑商公会联手主办 “你的电脑，他的希望”二手电脑捐赠计划，协助清贫学生得到二手电脑设备及平板电脑，方便居家网上学习，获得平等的教育机会。
