About Us

Founded in 1997, BOB Computer Services has been delivering reliable IT solutions to
homes, schools, universities, hospitals, manufacturers, and retailers.
Our offerings cover a wide range of computers, software, hardware, and accessories,
complemented by expert support, maintenance, and training services.

Why Choose BOB Computer Services?

With over 20 years of experience, we combine trusted IT expertise, quality products, and professional support to deliver solutions that help businesses and individuals succeed.

  • 01 Comprehensive IT Solutions

    From computers, hardware, and accessories to networking and maintenance services, we provide end-to-end solutions for homes, schools, and businesses.

  • 02 Authorized AutoCount Distributor

    As the authorized distributor of AutoCount Accounting software since 2016, we help companies of all sizes streamline operations with reliable accounting solutions.

  • 03 Training & Customer Support

    Our team goes beyond sales — we conduct training, seminars, and offer responsive support to ensure our clients gain maximum value from our solutions.
