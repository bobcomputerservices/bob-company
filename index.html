About Us

Founded in 1997, BOB Computer Services has been delivering reliable IT solutions
to universities, hospitals, manufacturers, and retailers.
Our offerings cover a wide range of computers, software, hardware, and accessories,
complemented by expert support, maintenance, and training services.

Why Choose BOB Computer Services?

  • 01 Decades of Expertise, Trusted for Reliability

    Approaching 30 years in the industry, we are recognized for our professionalism and integrity, delivering reliable solutions and services to our clients.

  • 02 Award-Winning AutoCount Dealer

    As the authorized dealer of AutoCount since 2011, we were recognized for growth and excellence. We were honored with the Top 10 Outstanding Business Growth Dealer Award in 2024, and have consistently received the Excellent Dealer Award for three consecutive years since 2022.

  • 03 Hardware to Support — All-in-One

    With comprehensive one-stop services covering computers, networking, and maintenance, we deliver reliable solutions that businesses and individuals can depend on.

  • 04 Training for Growth

    Our commitment extends beyond sales by offering training, seminars, and responsive support to help clients get the most from our solutions.
